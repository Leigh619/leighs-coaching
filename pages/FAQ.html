1. What results can I expect from coaching?

Most clients see improvements in strength, physique, and energy within the first 4–6 weeks. Physical changes vary by person, but the focus is on sustainable progress, not quick fixes.

. Do I need gym experience to start?

No. Many clients begin as complete beginners. Your programme is tailored to your level, equipment, and confidence — whether that’s a full gym or a few dumbbells at home.

3. How does online coaching work?

You get a personalised training plan, nutrition guidance, weekly check-ins, form feedback, and ongoing support through Whatsapp or Discord app. Everything is structured so you always know exactly what to do and why.

4. What’s included in the coaching?

✅Custom training programme ✅Nutrition guidance based on your lifestyle ✅Weekly check-ins ✅Video form reviews ✅Messaging support ✅Progress tracking ✅ Blood work analysis ✅ Sleep support

5. Do I need to track calories?

Not unless you want to. If you opt for a personalised meal plan you will not need to track calorie intake.

6. How long do I need to commit?

Most clients choose spend 5 months and more with me to build momentum and consistency, but you can continue month-to-month. Coaching is flexible and designed to fit your life.

7. What if I have injuries or limitations?

Your programme is adapted around any injuries, mobility issues, or confidence barriers. Everything is built to be safe, progressive, and achievable.

8. How do payments work?

Payments are taken monthly. There are no hidden fees, and you can cancel anytime with notice.

9. Will you help with nutrition as well as training?

Yes — you get evidence-based nutrition guidance tailored to your goals, preferences, and lifestyle. No restrictive diets or “clean eating” rules.
